Essential Baby Rules 📖

10 golden rules every new mother should know.

  1. 1Always place the baby on their back to sleep.
  2. 2Never shake a baby, even when overwhelmed.
  3. 3Feed regularly and monitor wet diapers.
  4. 4Wash hands before handling the baby.
  5. 5Never ignore a fever or breathing problems.
  6. 6Always use a rear-facing car seat.
  7. 7Never leave the baby unattended on high surfaces.
  8. 8Respond calmly to crying.
  9. 9Keep all doctor visits and vaccinations.
  10. 10Ask for help when exhausted.
