Essential Rules for New Mothers

  1. Always place the baby on their back to sleep.
  2. Never shake a baby, even when overwhelmed.
  3. Feed the baby regularly and monitor wet diapers.
  4. Wash hands before handling the baby.
  5. Do not ignore fever or breathing problems.
  6. Always use a rear-facing car seat.
  7. Never leave the baby unattended on high surfaces.
  8. Respond calmly to crying and discomfort.
  9. Keep regular doctor visits and vaccinations.
  10. Ask for help when physically or emotionally exhausted.
