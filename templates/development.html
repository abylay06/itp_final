Baby Growth Milestones

Month 1

  • Recognizes parents' voices
  • Sleeps most of the day
  • Begins making small sounds

Month 3

  • Smiles at people
  • Follows objects with eyes
  • Holds head up better

Month 6

  • Rolls over
  • Begins sitting with support
  • Responds to familiar faces

Month 9

  • Crawls or moves around
  • Understands simple words
  • May begin standing with support

Month 12

  • May take first steps
  • Says simple words
  • Waves goodbye
